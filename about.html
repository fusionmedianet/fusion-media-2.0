About Fusion Media

Connecting brands with the right audience

Our Story

Hi, I'm Alem Hodžić, the founder of Fusion Media — a modern influencer marketing agency I started in November 2024. I'm 17 years old, currently a high school student, and a young entrepreneur with a deep passion for the online world.

I began working online as a freelancer when I was just 13 years old. Since then, I've learned a lot about digital business and built experience in different areas. Over time, I became especially interested in influencer marketing, and that's what led me to create Fusion Media.

Our Approach

Right now, I'm learning directly from experienced mentors in this field, which gives me the confidence to deliver strong results for every client I work with. I may be young, but I take every project seriously and always give my best.

At Fusion Media, my goal is simple — to help brands in gaming and tech spaces grow by connecting them with the right influencers. I believe in honest work, creative ideas, and building long-term relationships.

Our Vision

This is just the beginning, and I'm excited for what's ahead. We're building a network of trusted influencers and brands that share our values of authenticity and quality.

Our vision is to become the go-to agency for brands looking to make a real impact in the digital space, through strategic partnerships with influencers who truly resonate with their audience.
